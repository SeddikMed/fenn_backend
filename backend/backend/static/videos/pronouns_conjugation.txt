Vidéo de conjugaison des pronoms :

Règles :
- "I" utilise "have" (jamais "has")
- "He/She/It" utilise "has" (avec un 's')
- "You/We/They" utilisent "have" (sans 's')

Exemples :
- I have a book
- He has a car
- She has a house
- It has four legs
- We have time
- You have many friends
- They have a meeting
